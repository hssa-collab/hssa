About Us

Who are we?

We are an umbrella student association for all health sciences faculties/programs, aiming to bring student perspectives to the college and develop opportunities for students to learn about other disciplines in healthcare during their time in university.

What do we do?

Interprofessional collaboration at the student level is key to the future work we all will do in healthcare to create a stronger system that is based on collaboration and respect, inclusivity, and teamwork. We understand the importance of relational work across disciplines, which we aim to foster to promote efficiency in the future workforce. We play an integral role in connecting the health sciences faculty associations and developing interprofessional opportunities for students.

Vision Statement

We aim to build a connected and collaborative community of health sciences students that champions interprofessional learning, fosters respectful and inclusive relationships across disciplines, and strengthens the future of healthcare through unity and shared purpose.

Areas of focus:

  • Working with the college to ensure quality interdisciplinary education
  • Represent students’ perspectives to the college on issues that affect them directly
  • Bring students from different faculties together in educational or entertaining events
  • Support our faculty associations within the college with outreach and facilitating collaborations
